about Faridah

My name is Faridah and I am a women's health writer. I live in Nigeria and I am committed to clarifying health misinformation , one quality and one well-researched write-up at a time.

I've always had a passion for writing...I LOVE WRITING!!!! I believe in the power of a pen, especially about health.

Martin Luther King said "If you want to change the world, pick up your pen and write!".
